## ALGEBRA

### Q1.
If the roots of the equation  
x² − (a + 2)x + a = 0  
are real and positive, then the range of a is ______.

**Answer:** (0, 4]

---

### Q2.
The number of real solutions of  
|x² − 5x + 6| = |x − 2|  
is ______.

**Answer:** 3

---

### Q3.
If α and β are the roots of  
x² − 4x + 1 = 0,  
then the value of  
α³ + β³  
is ______.

**Answer:** 52

---

### Q4.
The number of integral solutions of  
x² − y² = 15  
is ______.

**Answer:** 8

---

### Q5.
Let A = {1, 2, 3, 4}.  
The number of **onto functions** from A to A is ______.

**Answer:** 9

---

## PROBABILITY

### Q6.
Two dice are thrown. The probability that the sum of numbers is a **prime number** is ______.

**Answer:** 5/12

---

### Q7.
A coin is tossed 5 times. The probability of getting **at least one head** is ______.

**Answer:** 31/32

---

### Q8.
Three cards are drawn at random from a deck of 52 cards. The probability that all three are **face cards** is ______.

**Answer:** 11/850

---

### Q9.
A die is thrown repeatedly until a six appears. The probability that the six appears **for the first time on the third throw** is ______.

**Answer:** 25/216

---

### Q10.
If P(A) = 0.6, P(B) = 0.5 and P(A ∩ B) = 0.3, then P(A | B) is ______.

**Answer:** 0.6
