
# JEE / EAMCET Practice Questions (Algebra, Probability, Calculus, Linear Algebra)

**Difficulty Mix:** Easy / Medium / Hard (JEE-style, not Olympiad)

## Algebra

### Q1 (Easy)
Solve for x:  
2x + 5 = 13  

**Answer:** x = 4

---

### Q2 (Easy)
If a + b = 7 and ab = 10, find a² + b².

**Answer:** 29

---

### Q3 (Medium)
Find the roots of the equation:  
x² − 5x + 6 = 0

**Answer:** x = 2, 3

---

### Q4 (Medium)
If x + 1/x = 5, find x² + 1/x².

**Answer:** 23

---

### Q5 (Hard)
Solve: |2x − 3| = |x + 4|

**Answer:** x = −1/3, x = 7

---

## Probability

### Q6 (Easy)
A die is rolled once. What is the probability of getting an even number?

**Answer:** 1/2

---

### Q7 (Easy)
A coin is tossed twice. Find the probability of getting exactly one head.

**Answer:** 1/2

---

### Q8 (Medium)
Two cards are drawn from a deck without replacement. Find the probability both are aces.

**Answer:** 1/221

---

### Q9 (Medium)
What is the probability of getting a sum of 8 when two dice are thrown?

**Answer:** 5/36

---

### Q10 (Hard)
A bag contains 5 red and 3 blue balls. Two balls are drawn randomly. Probability both are red?

**Answer:** 5/14

---

## Basic Calculus

### Q11 (Easy)
Find the limit:  
lim(x→0) (sin x)/x

**Answer:** 1

---

### Q12 (Easy)
Differentiate:  
f(x) = 3x² − 4x + 1

**Answer:** f′(x) = 6x − 4

---

### Q13 (Medium)
Find dy/dx if y = x³ + 5x² − 7

**Answer:** dy/dx = 3x² + 10x

---

### Q14 (Medium)
Find the slope of the curve y = x² at x = 2.

**Answer:** 4

---

### Q15 (Hard)
Find the maximum value of y = −x² + 4x + 1.

**Answer:** 5

---
